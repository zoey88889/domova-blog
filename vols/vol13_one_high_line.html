One High Line stands as an architectural duet at the convergence of Chelsea and Hudson Yards. Designed by Bjarke Ingels Group (BIG), its two twisting glass towers redefine the skyline with sculptural elegance and contemporary edge.

Located above the famed High Line Park and steps from the Hudson River, this mixed-use development combines luxury residences with a Faena-branded hotel, all while preserving distinct private entrances and amenity zones for residents.

  • Directly above High Line, facing the Hudson River
  • Dramatic dual-tower geometry by BIG
  • Penthouses with private terraces and pools
  • Dedicated hotel & residential zones
  • Full suite of wellness and social amenities
