111 Murray Street｜111 默里街

地址：111 Murray Street, New York, NY 10007

项目介绍

111 Murray Street 是一座优雅高耸的摩天住宅，由 Kohn Pedersen Fox 与室内设计师 David Mann 合作打造。其独特的瓶形轮廓在 TriBeCa 区域极具辨识度，风格现代流畅，是城市天际线的闪耀明星。

建筑特色

  • 高层住宅可俯瞰曼哈顿、哈德逊河与自由女神像
  • 三层豪华配套空间包括 SPA、50 英尺泳池、健身中心、儿童乐园、私人放映厅
  • 玻璃幕墙带来自然光充沛与极致视野

周边配套

步行可至世贸中心、布鲁克菲尔德广场、高端购物与地铁交通枢纽，生活便利且充满国际都市感。

英文版 / English Version

111 Murray Street

Address: 111 Murray Street, New York, NY 10007

Introduction

111 Murray Street is a striking tower designed by KPF and interior designer David Mann. Its curved, sculptural silhouette rises elegantly over Tribeca, commanding attention in Manhattan’s skyline.

Features

  • Panoramic views of Manhattan, the Hudson River, and the Statue of Liberty
  • Three floors of amenities: spa, 50-foot pool, fitness center, kids’ playroom, and screening room
  • Floor-to-ceiling glass windows maximize sunlight and views
