The Cortland｜科特兰大楼

地址：555 West 22nd Street, New York, NY 10011

项目介绍

The Cortland 位于切尔西西区，是由 Robert A.M. Stern Architects 设计的经典之作，融合了传统工艺与现代建筑美学。其外立面采用优雅的红砖和石材，内部设计则精致优雅，营造出温馨又尊贵的居住体验。

建筑特色

  • 奢华设施：包括游泳池、水疗中心、健身房、儿童娱乐室及宠物专属空间
  • 挑高大堂，定制雕花木饰与艺术装置
  • 部分户型配备露台，坐拥哈德逊河景

周边配套

距离High Line高线公园仅几步之遥，邻近Hudson River Park与Chelsea艺术画廊区，艺术氛围浓厚，生活便利。

The Cortland

Address: 555 West 22nd Street, New York, NY 10011

Introduction

The Cortland, located in West Chelsea, is a masterwork designed by Robert A.M. Stern Architects. The façade features a combination of hand-laid brick and limestone, with interiors that evoke refined craftsmanship and serene luxury.

Features

  • Luxurious amenities including pool, spa, gym, kids’ playroom, and pet care space
  • Grand lobby with artisan woodwork and bespoke artwork
  • Some units offer private terraces with Hudson River views

Neighborhood

Steps away from the High Line, Hudson River Park, and renowned Chelsea galleries — the perfect blend of tranquility and creativity.
