70 Vestry Street｜威士忒街 70号

地址：70 Vestry Street, New York, NY 10013

项目介绍

70 Vestry Street 坐落于曼哈顿下城 Tribeca 区的滨水地段，由知名建筑师 Robert A.M. Stern 设计，整体建筑风格优雅庄重，充满经典魅力。

建筑特色

  • 外立面采用法国石灰石打造，典雅低调
  • 三层会所设施包括游泳池、水疗中心、图书室、餐厅休息区与儿童游乐室
  • 可享哈德逊河壮丽景观，部分户型带露台

周边配套

位于 Tribeca 北端，步行可至哈德逊河公园、SOHO、地铁站与各大高级餐厅，是名流雅士偏爱的静谧居所。

70 Vestry Street

Address: 70 Vestry Street, New York, NY 10013

Introduction

70 Vestry Street is an iconic waterfront condominium in Tribeca, designed by Robert A.M. Stern. Its classical limestone façade and refined interiors offer timeless elegance in one of Manhattan’s most sought-after neighborhoods.

Features

  • French limestone façade with understated luxury
  • Club amenities include pool, spa, library, dining suite, and children’s playroom
  • Sweeping views of the Hudson River; many units offer private terraces

Neighborhood

Located at the northern edge of Tribeca, steps from Hudson River Park, world-class dining, shopping, and major subway lines — an ideal mix of tranquility and accessibility.
