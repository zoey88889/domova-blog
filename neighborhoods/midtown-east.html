English Overview

Midtown East is where business meets diplomacy. Anchored by the United Nations Headquarters, this area is home to iconic towers, global embassies, and luxury skyscrapers. With Grand Central Terminal nearby and a skyline defined by Chrysler and modern glass architecture, this neighborhood reflects power, structure, and international elegance.
