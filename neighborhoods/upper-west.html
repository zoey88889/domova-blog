⭐ Upper West Side

“文艺灵魂的静谧绿洲”

English Overview

The Upper West Side is a leafy, cultured haven nestled between Central Park and the Hudson River. It’s where artists, academics, and old New York souls dwell — a neighborhood rich in pre-war brownstones, classical music, and intellectual flair. It’s slower, quieter, and full of timeless charm.

中文赏析

上西区，是一位披着长风衣的文艺灵魂。它被中央公园与哈德逊河温柔环绕， 孕育出优雅的古老公寓、咖啡馆里的剧作家，以及图书馆中翻阅世界的人们。 这里，是纽约的绿意后花园，也是知识分子的隐秘栖息地。
