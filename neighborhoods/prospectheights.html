English Overview

Prospect Heights is a charming, quieter gem in central Brooklyn, nestled between bustling Downtown and leafy Prospect Park. Known for its tree-lined avenues, historic brownstones, and cultural hotspots like the Brooklyn Museum and Barclays Center, it blends calm residential life with vibrant city energy.
